Moderate Cancellation Policy

Moderate Cancellation available up to 24 hours before rental starts. Charge will be pending from the time the user submits their reservation and will be processed 24 hours before rental starts. Late cancellation charges full amount for the time agreed on up to 24 hours. Service fees and applicable taxes for the full length of the original reservation are still due. Reservations are officially cancelled when the user clicks the “Cancel Reservation” button on the cancellation confirmation page If rental is returned early, the remaining reservation time up to 24 hours is still due. LendIt will mediate when necessary and retains final say in all disputes which may arise.
