Strict Cancellation Policy

Strict Cancellation available up to 48 hours before rental starts. Charge will be pending from the time the user submits their reservation and will be processed 48 hours before rental starts. Late cancellation charges full amount for the time agreed on up to 24 hours. Service fees and applicable taxes for the full length of the original reservation are still due. Reservations are officially cancelled when the user clicks the “Cancel Reservation” button on the cancellation confirmation page If the rental is returned early, remaining rental time up to 72 hours is still due. LendIt will mediate when necessary and retains final stay in all disputes which may arise.
